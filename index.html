Why you need MyDay

MyDay delivers a low risk, simple and flexible way to leverage existing investment in your VLE and Microsoft Office 365 by providing access to information via a highly intuitive and modern-styled interface.

By offering easy access to existing information on internal and external systems, MyDay helps to improve student engagement and helps facilitate work-based learning.

In particular it will help with many of the IT concerns expressed by the UCISA community:

Cloud, managed services and alternative delivery models

MyDay is built on Microsoft Azure and Office 365. This enables rapid delivery, easy scaling, low cost of implementation and self-service capabilities to reduce running costs.

Use of technology in student's teaching

Leverage existing investment in your VLE to deliver its capabilities direct to a student's device.

Mobile computing, anytime, anywhere with home working

Students and staff can access all their important information from any device with a Web browser and Internet connection.

Providing a quality and resilient service

With 99% uptime guaranteed, students and staff will always be able to access the information they need irrespective of availability of on premise infrastructure.

Delivering services under severe financial restraint

Low cost and flexible subscription-based service means there is no need for large capital up-front investment. Additionally, consultancy led setup and deployment with no development required reduces onboarding time and cost.

Want to improve student engagement?

With the competition for students intensifying for all academic institutions, the focus on student experience has increased over the last few years. Key to this has been improving student engagement as a variety of research has shown that engaged students:

  • Are less likely to dropout
  • Achieve higher grades
  • Perform better in exams and assessments
  • Have a greater sense of belonging
  • Are better able to set and meet personal goals
  • Persist on tasks

Whilst wider economic restrictions and technological limitations can appear to restrict the capability to improve student engagement it is still possible to achieve rapid and cost-effective progress in this area.

MyDay – easy access to information

This is because most of the information that can be used to improve student engagement already exists in a variety of both internal and external systems – all that needs to be done is to find a simple and cost-effective way of bringing them together.

MyDay is a proven world-class information delivery platform for educational establishments. By offering easy access to existing information on internal and external systems, it helps to improve student engagement and increase attendance, retention and attainment.

Impacting the factors that influence student engagement

By presenting their entire academic life in front of them it directly impacts the 4 areas that influence student engagement:

  • Academic
  • Behavioural
  • Cognitive
  • Affective engagement

It pulls together all of the disparate elements that make up a student's experience into one place and enables them to engage quickly with anything that is going on within your institution.

Your student life in one place

The unique and bold tile design of the MyDay interface gives users an at-a-glance view of their important information and notifications. They can then quickly and easily drill down into more detailed information by simply clicking a tile to open a detailed window. Some of the key tiles are shown below:

Key features

My Calendar

Current and upcoming events listed in your calendar

My Mail

Displays most recent e-mails

My ePayments

Shows current balance and recent transactions

My Alerts

All your latest alerts displayed in one tile

My VLE

Next activity due plus count of overdue activities

My Library

Next book due back and any overdue items

Additional features

Some of the other tiles available on the MyDay Platform include:

  • My Attendance - User's attendance for a defined period
  • My Learning Plan - The next goal or task that must be completed
  • My Print - Current print balance
  • My Photos - Thumbnails of images in your photo library
  • My Course - Learning objectives of the week
  • My Linker - Picture and small amount of user definable text
  • SkyDrive Pro - Access to SkyDrive Pro account area
  • My Site Newsfeed - Summary of recent activity from their MySite newsfeed
  • My Newsfeed - Recent news items from subscribed newsfeeds
  • Advert Rotator - Banner adverts stored in a picture library

Architecture benefits

  • Rapid delivery - consultancy led setup and deployment with no development required and a flexible API for fast customisation
  • Easy scaling - multi-tenant product that is scalable for all the estabilishment sizes, both nationally and internationally
  • Low cost of implementation - requires virtually no on-premise infrastructure
  • Self-service capabilities - reduces management and support overhead on institution
